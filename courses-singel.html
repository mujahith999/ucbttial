Course Summery

The Psychology & Counselling course provides a basic understanding of human behavior, mental health, and counseling techniques. It covers topics such as psychological theories, counseling methods, mental health disorders, and ethical considerations. Students will develop essential skills like active listening, empathy, and problem-solving, which are valuable for those interested in mental health, social work, or personal development .

Requrements

This course is open to anyone interested in psychology and counseling. No prior experience is required, but having strong communication skills, empathy, and an interest in human behavior will be beneficial. A high school diploma or equivalent is recommended. For online learning, students should have access to a computer or mobile device with a stable internet connection .
